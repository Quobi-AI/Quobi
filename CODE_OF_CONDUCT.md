# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be decent. Treat people with respect, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the work. Harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination are not welcome here.

## What we expect

- Be respectful and constructive, especially when you disagree.
- Give and accept feedback in good faith.
- Keep it on topic.

## What is not okay

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discrimination or demeaning comments about who someone is.
- Posting other people's private information.
- Sustained disruption of discussion.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and anywhere you are representing the project.

## Reporting

If you see or experience a problem, report it privately to a maintainer (you can reach the maintainers through their GitHub profiles on the organization page). Reports stay private. Maintainers will review and respond, and may warn, remove content, or ban anyone who breaks these rules.

This is adapted from the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
